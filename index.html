Where it goes

We keep what we can out of the landfill.

Usable furniture and appliances get set aside for donation, not dumped. We sort loads where we can and take everything to permitted facilities — the county-compliant way, in line with California's SB 1383 diversion rules. Less waste in the ground, same flat price to you.
