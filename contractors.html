Questions

        
What people ask us every day

      


      

        

          How long can I keep the dumpster?
          
Most people keep it a few days to a week — it's included in the flat rate. Need longer? Tell us your timeline and we'll make it work.

        

        

          Can I put it on the street?
          
Sometimes, depending on city and street rules. If you need street placement, we'll tell you exactly what to check for permits.

        

        

          What can't go in the dumpster?
          
Hazardous waste (chemicals, paint), batteries, and certain appliances need special handling. Not sure? Text us a photo and we'll tell you straight.

        

        

          Do you deliver same-day?
          
Yes. We answer around the clock and make same-day happen. Text or call with your ZIP, size, and debris type.
